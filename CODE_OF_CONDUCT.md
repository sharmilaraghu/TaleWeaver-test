# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

TaleWeaver is a storytelling app built for children. Everyone who contributes to this project — regardless of age, background, experience level, gender, nationality, religion, or identity — deserves a respectful and welcoming environment.

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**
- Be kind and respectful in all interactions
- Give and receive constructive feedback gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the project and its young users
- Acknowledge mistakes, learn from them, and move on
- Assume good intent from fellow contributors

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks of any kind
- Trolling or deliberately disruptive behavior
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting
- Contributing content that is harmful, unsafe, or inappropriate for children

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces — GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other forum where contributors represent the project.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening a private issue or contacting the maintainers directly. All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with discretion.

Maintainers reserve the right to remove comments, close issues, or ban contributors who violate this Code of Conduct.
